<div style="text-align: center;">
<h1>Mistress of Death</h1>
</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
A war inside me<br>
The sun over me<br>
Regrets behind me<br>
And You,<br>
in front of me<br>
Pale and beautiful<br>
<br>
And silence.<br>
<br>
In that moment<br>
no fears,<br>
no regrets,<br>
no wrath,<br>
I was free<br>
and in peace<br>
The only sound<br>
the low hum<br>
of a slowing heart.<br>
</div>
